DC Motor Control Systems

Using MATLAB and Simulink to simulate a DC motor controller, the control system of a DC motor was extensively analyzed. Using PID controllers and changing parameters of the load and the motor, each variable was extensively analyzed concluded in thorough understanding of control systems.

Transistor Analysis

The analysis of transistors and other components has occurred repeatedly throughout Cal Poly's courses. There has been extensive study about the steady state response, voltage gains, rise and fall times, and filters. Of the circuits studied, Op Amps, BJTs, JFETs, MOSFETs, Capacitors, Inductors, Resistors, along with rectifiers, filters, and generators have been analyzed.

Circuit Logic

Digital Integrated Circuit Design was extensively used during a course at Cal Poly. In some instances, Verilog was used to integrate computer logic into circuits. In other instances, multiplexers, decoders, gates and other hardware was used to simulate hardware computer logic. Both cases helped instruct software and hardware circuit logic and design.
